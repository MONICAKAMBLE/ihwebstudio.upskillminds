About Me

"As a corporate trainer and coach with over 23 years of experience in the travel industry, I have honed my skills in leadership, team building, sales, operations, customer service, and more. My extensive exposure to international destinations and cultures, coupled with my passion for building effective relationships, has helped me drive customer-centricity and business profitability through strategic initiatives.

In the past 8 years, I have transformed close to 5000+ individuals from different sectors to navigate and be successful in their professional journey. My hands-on experience in the travel industry, combined with my skills in facilitation and training conceptualization, design, and delivery, has helped me develop training programs that are different and effective.

My journey from the travel industry to corporate training and coaching has enabled me to bridge the gap between academics and corporate needs. I understand the challenges that freshers face in being corporate-ready, and my training programs aim to equip them with the skills and knowledge required to succeed in their careers.

Whether you are an individual looking to enhance your professional skills or a company seeking to transform your workforce, I am confident that my unique blend of hands-on experience and training expertise can help you achieve your goals.

Bhavani Arun
